# How Nature Inspires Creativity

Artists, writers, and musicians often draw inspiration from the natural world. The colors, shapes, and sounds found outdoors can influence creative work and provide a fresh perspective.

-  Take photos or sketch scenes to deepen your appreciation.
-  Learn about local plants and wildlife.
